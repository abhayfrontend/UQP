Gap Identification & Closure

QAPRIMA is designed to provide a complete quality suite in one convenient, easy-to-use application. It provides the ability to quickly identify under-performance right on the home page so work can begin without delay. The user-friendly design allows for quick and easy use, even for inexperienced users, and the work flow is designed in such a way to reduce the potential for data entry errors.

Performance and Productivity Tracking

QAPRIMA's unique ability to track performance and productivity creates an opportunity for organizations to easily and effectively measure and monitor their providers’ and employees’ work. This data can be used to identify trends and shortfalls, revealing areas in need of retraining and, ultimately, leading to a visible, measurable improvement in quality and outcomes.
